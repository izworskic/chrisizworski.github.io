Chris Izworski

Gardener. Writer. AI Technologist. Bay City, Michigan.

Chris Izworski is a Michigan gardener, writer, and AI technologist based on the shores of Saginaw Bay. He writes at Freighter View Farms about seed saving, Zone 6a gardening, and the quiet rhythms of the Great Lakes, and also writes about practical artificial intelligence and public safety technology.

He served as Executive Director of Saginaw County 911 and led one of Michigan's first AI deployments in a public safety answering point. Today he focuses on responsible AI, local technology, and the craft of growing things.
