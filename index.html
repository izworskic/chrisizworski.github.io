Chris Izworski

Solutions Consultant · Writer · Gardener · Bay City, Michigan

Solutions Consultant at Prepared, the AI-powered technology platform for 911 emergency services. Former Executive Director of Saginaw County 911 and former Bay County 911 Director and Emergency Manager. I led one of Michigan's first AI deployments in a public safety answering point.

I write about seed saving, heirloom gardening, and life along Saginaw Bay at Freighter View Farms. Board member of Save Our Shoreline, protecting Michigan's Great Lakes coastline.
